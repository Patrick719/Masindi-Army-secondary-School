Our MASSOSA Network

The Masindi Army SS Old Students Association brings together former students from different graduating classes. Our aim is to create professional networks, organize reunions, support current students, and celebrate the heritage of our school.

Activities & Benefits

  • Annual alumni reunions
  • Career mentorship programs
  • Networking opportunities
  • Support for school initiatives
